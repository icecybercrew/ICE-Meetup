# 10 May 2025

## Summary
This meetup was organized by [SIG: Cybersecurity IIUM](https://www.linkedin.com/company/sigcyberseciium/). The session focused on Binary Exploitation, where participants were introduced to core concepts such as stack behavior, memory layout, and buffer overflow attacks. Special thanks to [Bro Capang](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gnapac/)  for breaking down the fundamentals of binary exploitation in an easy-to-understand and engaging way. His walkthrough covered both theory and practical exploitation techniques, sparking a lot of interest and discussion among the attendees.
